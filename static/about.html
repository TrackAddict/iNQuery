About iNQuery

iNQuery is a web-hosted application designed to serve as a quick and accurate reference for evaluationg political officials and candidates. iNQuery draws on official records, natural language processing (NLP), and multiple third party-evaluators to deliver data-driven and accessible representations of the the demonstrated policies and records of federal legislators and state governors. iNQuery also offers tools for immediately accessible comparisons between officials in the same branch of Congress.

How to use iNQuery

When you navigate to iNQuery, you will see several options on the front page to help you refine your search. iNQuery currently offer records and evaluations of U.S. Federal Legislators and State Governors, with additional functionality allowing for direct comparison of legislators in the same house of Congress, along with access to NLP-driven summaries of each bill considered and how each legislator voted on those bills. iNQuery also provides access to voter and single-issue advocacy ratings for state governors.
